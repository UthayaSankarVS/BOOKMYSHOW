About the Movie


Amaran tells the inspiring story of Major Mukund Varadarajan, an Indian Army officer honoured with the Ashok Chakra for his heroic actions during a counterterrorism operation in Jammu and Kashmir. The film also highlights the unwavering love and sacrifice of his wife, Indhu Rebecca Varghese, serving as a heartfelt tribute to the courage of soldiers and their families.
